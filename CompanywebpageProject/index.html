Our Programs

Infant (6 weeks to 18 months)

Our infant program provides loving care and educational enrichment to all of our youngest students. With daily tummy time, outside time, and teacher-led activities designed to enhance your child's development.you can be sure your child is in the best hands.

Toddler (18 months to 36 months)

Our toddler program packed full of engaging activities, physical play, and the foundations for learning. Children in this group begin to learn their letters, shapes, colors, and more! Our curriculum follows a science-backed program designed with your toddler's growing brain in mind!

Preschool (3-4 years)

Our preschool program is exciting, engaging, and full of fun! Our curriculum is designed to prepare your child for Pre-K and beyond! Your child will learn to write their name, count to ten, and much much more!

Pre-K (4-5 years)

Our Pre-K program is designed in conjunction with the local Kindergarten programs so that you can rest easy knowing your child is prepared for the next step: Kindergarten! Your child will learn to read and write, the foundations for STEM and much more!
